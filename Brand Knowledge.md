## Tone of Voice

**Overall character:** Professional but warm. Never dry or corporate.

The brand speaks like a reliable partner, not a distant supplier. The tone is confident, approachable, and focused on the client's benefit — always making it easy to take the next step.

**Key principles:**

- **Warm and partnership-oriented** — talk to the client as a collaborator, not a buyer. Use "we" and "our clients" naturally.
- **Confident, not boastful** — state facts and experience plainly. Let quality speak for itself.
- **Simple and direct** — avoid jargon. Sentences are clear and easy to scan.
- **Helpful and action-oriented** — end with an invitation: "feel free to contact us", "our managers will help you choose".
- **No stiff formality** — avoid overly formal or bureaucratic language. Keep it human.

**Tone in practice (from approved copy):**

> *"At Stelvi we are always happy to bring your ideas to life, mix tastes, and create new designs."*

> *"We're always ready to help you and answer your questions."*

> *"Feel free to contact us by using the form below!"*

**What to avoid:**
- Cold, transactional language
- Overpromising superlatives without substance
- Technical jargon without explanation

## Shokobox

### Idea & Concept

The Shokobox symbol is inspired by a classic chocolate bar forming the shape of the letter **"S"**. Simple, minimalistic, contemporary logo reflecting clean design, quality, and modern aesthetics.

---

### Symbol

The Shokobox symbol is a stylized letter **S** built from rectangular blocks resembling chocolate bar segments. It carries a registered trademark mark (®).

**Versions:**
- Full-size symbol (primary)
- Smaller variant (for reduced sizes)

---

### Logotype

The logotype combines the **symbol + "Shokobox" wordmark** in a clean sans-serif typeface. The pairing creates a balance between modern minimalism and approachability.

**Versions:**
- Horizontal (symbol + text in one line)
- Vertical (symbol above text)

---

### Clear Space

**Symbol:** The clear space is defined by the size of one square element of the symbol. An equal margin — one block unit — must surround the symbol on all sides.

**Logotype (horizontal):** The clear space is defined by the height of the symbol. An equal margin — one symbol height — must surround the full logotype on all sides.

> No other elements — text, images, or graphics — may appear within the clear space zone.

---

### Minimum Size

| Version | Minimum width |
|---|---|
| Horizontal logotype | 20 mm |
| Vertical logotype | 10 mm |

Below 20 mm, the horizontal logotype must not be used — only the vertical version is acceptable.
